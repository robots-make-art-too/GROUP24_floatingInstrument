Floating Game

Welcome to our page, where our group "Floating Game" aims to have an AR that is almost game-like, to show how we can still have fun and provide viewers with entertainment and the thought of social distancing.

Pressing the Click Here button will take you to our AR page.

Once you have activated the AR camera, you will have to travel around Toronto and find our specific markers to unlock something special!
